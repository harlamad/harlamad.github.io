Crash N' Dash Tacos

Welcom to Crash N' Dash Tacos, the object of the game is to hit that green car with your tacos by pressing the space bar and to push the left and right arrow keys to rotate. You only have 20 sec. before time runs out. If you wish to go fast then push the 'Play Again Faster' button. Its harder then it looks so good luck. Please do not hold the space bar or you might break the game.
